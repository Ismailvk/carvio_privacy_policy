Privacy Policy for Carvio
Information We Collect

When you use Carvio, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details. This information is used to create and manage your account.

To facilitate car bookings, we may collect details such as pickup/drop-off locations, reservation times, and other relevant booking information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal and booking information to provide you with the requested car booking services.

Your data helps us understand your preferences and improve our app's functionality and user interface.

We may use your contact information to send you booking confirmations, updates . You can opt-out of promotional communications at any time.

Information Sharing

We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate bookings, payment processing, and other essential services.

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to legal requests.

Location Permission

To enhance your experience with Carvio, our app may request access to your device's location. This permission is utilized for providing location-based services, allowing you to seamlessly book vehicles and find nearby rental locations.

We want to assure you that we respect your privacy, and the location permission is solely for the purpose of optimizing the functionality of Carvio. We do not access, read, or transfer any personal or sensitive information without your explicit consent.

Your data's security is a priority for us, and we implement measures to safeguard it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Payment Processing with Razorpay

Carvio utilizes Razorpay, a secure third-party payment gateway, for processing payments when you book a vehicle. When you initiate a payment transaction, certain payment-related information, such as your payment method details and transaction details, may be collected and processed by Razorpay to facilitate the transaction.

We want to emphasize that the handling of your payment information is conducted securely through Razorpay's platform. Carvio does not store or have access to your sensitive payment details, ensuring a high level of data security during the payment process.

Razorpay's privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policy to understand how they handle your payment information. You can find Razorpay's privacy policy on their official website.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the payment process or the handling of your payment information, please contact us at wheelsondemandwod@gmail.com.

Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.

You can opt-out of promotional communications.

Coupon Application:

Carvio allows users to apply coupons during the booking process. Once a coupon is successfully applied, it cannot be applied again for subsequent bookings. This ensures fair usage and prevents duplicate coupon application.
